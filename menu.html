Our Menu

Alfredo Pasta

Indulge in the creamy perfection of our Alfredo Pasta, crafted with a velvety parmesan cheese sauce, perfectly cooked fettuccine, and a hint of garlic. Topped with fresh parsley, this classic dish is comfort food at its finest. Add grilled chicken or shrimp for an extra burst of flavor.

Price: $11

Moroccan Chicken

A taste of Morocco on your plate! Our Moroccan Chicken is a rich and aromatic dish, featuring tender, spiced chicken marinated in a blend of warm spices and slow-cooked to perfection. Served with a side of herbed couscous and roasted vegetables for a wholesome, flavorful meal.

Price: $12

Olivers Club Sandwich

A classic favorite with a twist! Our Club Sandwich is stacked high with layers of roasted chicken, crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and creamy mayonnaise, all tucked between perfectly toasted bread. Served with a side of golden fries or a fresh salad.

Price: $10

Mint Margharita

Cool, refreshing, and absolutely invigorating! Our Mint Margarita is a zesty blend of fresh mint leaves, tangy lime juice, and a touch of sweetness, served chilled with a hint of fizz. The perfect drink to uplift your mood any time of the day.

Price: $4

Creme Brule

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this timeless French dessert. Our Crème Brûlée features a silky, creamy custard base topped with a perfectly caramelized sugar crust. Crack into the golden topping and savor every spoonful of this delightful treat.

Price: $5
